Is It Worth Fixing an Electric Scooter?

In most cases, fixing an electric scooter is worth it only if the repair costs less than half the price of a replacement. Batteries and controllers are usually the deciding factor.

When fixing usually makes sense

  • Flat tires or worn brakes
  • Loose cables or minor electrical issues
  • Parts costing under $75

When replacing is usually smarter

  • Battery replacement on older models
  • Controller plus wiring issues
  • Cracked frames or structural damage
